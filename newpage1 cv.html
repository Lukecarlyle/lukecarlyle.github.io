YOUNG THUG

Young Thug is one of my favorite rappers of all time. From when Young Thug released his first major album "1017 Thug" I became a huge fan. He then released his record breaking album JEFFERY. Several great songs came from that album my favorites are Wyclef Jean, Guwop and Webbie. Thug recently released Beautiful Thugger Girls which is one of my top listen to albums of 2017.
